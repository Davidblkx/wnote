WNote - whatsapp as personal notes

Did you know you can use WhatsApp as a personal notes app? Just add your own number, pin the chat, and send messages to it whenever you want to save a note. You can also use it to share notes across your devices.

Enter your number below in the international format (without the leading 00 or +), press 'Generate Link,' and use the QR code or link to open it in WhatsApp
